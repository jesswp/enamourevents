About Us

Fall In Love With The I Do's and To Do's

En Amour Events is an Ottawa boutique event company that caters to fun and modern couples looking to have the best day of their lives. If timeless, elegant, and lavish describe your dream wedding, you have come to the right place, and we should seriously consider popping some bubbly to celebrate!

Fuelled by the goosebumps from the epic moments in every event, we are inspired to do much more than create a dream wedding. We are here to create a unique and memorable celebration that shows who you are, and what your life together is all about.
